Reebok EasyTone case study

Reimagining Reebok's EasyTone line for men, with a focus on busy fathers between the ages of 35 and 50, was the task assigned for a college marketing project. The assignment looked for to raise awareness of a 25% discount offer while removing the stigma associated with men wearing EasyTone. Understanding the time limitations that contemporary fathers face as a result of their work and family obligations was necessary to create a campaign that complemented their way of life.
